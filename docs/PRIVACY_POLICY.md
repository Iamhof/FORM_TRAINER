# Privacy Policy for Form - Workout Tracker

**Last Updated:** January 18, 2026

## Introduction

Form - Workout Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Email Address:** Used for account creation and authentication
- **Name:** Used to personalize your experience
- **Profile Settings:** Including accent color preferences

### Workout Data
- **Training Programmes:** Your custom workout programmes
- **Workout History:** Logged exercises, sets, reps, and weights
- **Exercise Selections:** Exercises you've added to your programmes
- **Timestamps:** When workouts are logged and completed

### Technical Data
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version
- **App Usage:** Features accessed, app crashes (anonymized)
- **Performance Data:** App performance metrics for improvement

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the app functionality
- Enable user authentication and account management
- Store and sync your workout data across devices
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Troubleshoot technical issues
- Communicate important updates about the app

## Data Storage and Security

### Third-Party Services

We use **Supabase** (supabase.com) as our database and authentication provider:
- Data is stored securely in the cloud
- Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
- Industry-standard security practices
- Supabase privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

### Security Measures
- All data transmitted using encryption (HTTPS)
- Secure authentication using industry-standard methods
- Password hashing and secure storage
- Regular security updates

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your workout data with other users (unless you explicitly opt into future social features)
- Use your data for advertising purposes

We may share data only when:
- Required by law or legal process
- Necessary to protect our rights or the safety of users
- With your explicit consent

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- **Correction:** Update or correct your personal information
- **Export:** Download your workout data

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@formworkout.app (or your support email)

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** Data retained while your account is active
- **Account Deletion:** All personal data deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
- **Backup Data:** Removed from backups within 90 days

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:
- Posting the new policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Sending an email notification (for material changes)

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
