1. Overview

Form - Workout Tracker (“Form”, “we”, “our”, or “us”), developed by OJ Gyms, provides a fitness tracking application for iOS. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use the Form mobile application (the “App”). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Email address and password for account creation and authentication.
  • Profile Information: Display name (if you opt into the leaderboard feature).
  • Workout Data: Training programmes, exercise selections, workout logs including sets, repetitions, weights, and timestamps.
  • Preferences: Accent colour preference, notification settings, and other in-app settings.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers required for app functionality.
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, session duration, and interaction patterns used solely to improve app performance.
  • Crash and Diagnostic Data: Anonymised crash reports, error logs, and performance metrics to identify and resolve technical issues.
  • Subscription Data: Purchase status and subscription tier (processed through Apple and RevenueCat; we do not collect or store payment card details).

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect health data from Apple HealthKit or any health framework.
  • We do not access your device camera, microphone, contacts, or location.
  • We do not collect biometric data.
  • We do not collect payment card numbers, bank account details, or financial information directly.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App. Each service processes data in accordance with its own privacy policy:

4.1 Supabase (Database and Authentication)

  • Purpose: Secure data storage, user authentication, and real-time data synchronisation.
  • Data Processed: Account information, workout data, user preferences.
  • Security: Data encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Industry-standard security practices.
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

4.2 RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

  • Purpose: Managing in-app subscriptions and purchase verification.
  • Data Processed: Anonymous user identifier, subscription status, purchase receipts (processed via Apple).
  • Note: We never receive or store your payment card information. All payment processing is handled by Apple.
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

4.3 Sentry (Error Monitoring)

  • Purpose: Crash reporting and performance monitoring to maintain app stability.
  • Data Processed: Anonymised crash reports, device type, OS version, stack traces.
  • Note: No personally identifiable information is intentionally sent to Sentry.
  • Privacy Policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/

4.4 Expo (App Framework and Updates)

  • Purpose: App distribution, over-the-air updates, and development infrastructure.
  • Data Processed: Device type, OS version, app version.
  • Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

4.5 Apple (App Store and Payments)

  • Purpose: App distribution, in-app purchase processing, and payment handling.
  • Data Processed: Apple manages all payment transactions. We receive only confirmation of subscription status.
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

5. Leaderboard Feature

The leaderboard is an optional, opt-in feature. If you choose to participate:

  • You will be asked to create a display name.
  • Your display name and anonymised workout performance metrics (e.g., total volume, workout count) will be visible to other opted-in users.
  • No personal information beyond your chosen display name is shared.
  • You may opt out of the leaderboard at any time through the App settings, which will remove your display name and metrics from public view.

If you do not opt in, no workout data is shared with other users.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Encryption at Rest: Data stored on our servers is encrypted at rest.
  • Secure Authentication: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms and are never stored in plain text.
  • Secure Token Storage: Authentication tokens are stored using platform-native secure storage (iOS Keychain via Expo SecureStore).
  • Access Controls: Server-side role-based access controls limit data access to authorised operations only.
  • Regular Updates: We regularly update our dependencies and infrastructure to address known security vulnerabilities.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Your data is retained for as long as your account remains active and you continue to use the App.
  • Account Deletion: Upon receiving a deletion request, we will delete all personal data associated with your account within 30 days. Anonymised data in backups may persist for up to 90 days before automatic deletion.
  • Inactive Accounts: Accounts with no activity for 24 months may be flagged for deletion. We will attempt to notify you via email before any action is taken.

  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated personal data.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for optional features (e.g., leaderboard) at any time.
  • Restrict Processing: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at benhofmeister3@gmail.com. We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.

8.1 Account Deletion

You may delete your account directly within the App by navigating to your profile settings. This will:

  • Permanently delete your account and login credentials.
  • Permanently delete all workout data, programmes, and preferences.
  • Remove your data from the leaderboard (if opted in).
  • Revoke all active sessions.

This action is irreversible.
